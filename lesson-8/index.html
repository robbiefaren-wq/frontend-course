Sadie Elizabeth Sink (born April 16, 2002) is an American actress. She began her career in theater, playing the title role in the musical Annie (2012–14) and young Elizabeth II in the historical play The Audience (2015) on Broadway. In 2016, she made her film debut in the biographical sports drama Chuck.

Key skills of Sadie Sink:

  • Acting – She is best known for her role as Max in the TV show Stranger Things. She can perform emotional and dramatic scenes very strongly.
  • Film acting – She starred in movies like The Whale, where she played complex dramatic roles.
  • Music video acting – She appeared in the short film/music video for All Too Well (10 Minute Version) by Taylor Swift.
