Ru YouTube

Ru YouTube - is an alternative Russian YouTube alternatives. We are not just new dumb servise. We are It’s the same YouTube, but without a VPN or restrictions. Our service already has a built-in VPN, and it’s completely safe. We want to help Russians in their fight to access the content they prefer.
